Artificial intelligence models can reflect biases present in the data used during training, 
which may influence the responses they generate. Because of this, AI outputs should not always be 
considered fully accurate or unbiased. Developers must be aware of these limitations and design 
applications that encourage responsible use of AI systems. In this project, the chatbot is intended
 only as a demonstration of AI integration and experimentation with natural language processing. 
 Users should verify important information from reliable sources rather than relying solely on the 
 AI’s responses. Responsible AI development includes transparency about the models used, awareness 
 of potential bias, and careful evaluation of generated outputs before applying them in real-world 
 contexts.
